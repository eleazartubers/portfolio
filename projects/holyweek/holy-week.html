HOLY WEEK TIMELINE

Day 1: On Sunday, Triumphal Entry on Palm Sunday
Day 2: On Monday, Jesus curse the fig tree and clears the Temple
Day 3: On Tuesday, Jesus Goes to the Mount of Olives
Day 4: Holy Wednesday, Jesus continues daily teaching in the Temple
Day 5: Passover and Last Supper on Maundy Thursday
Day 6: Trial, Crucifixion, Death and Burial on Good Friday
Day 7: Saturday in the Tomb, which was the Sabbath
Day 8: Resurrection Sunday, Empty-tomb witnesses
